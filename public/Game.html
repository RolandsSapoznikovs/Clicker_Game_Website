In the game, there is a tree in the middle of the screen, on which, when you click with the mouse, you will earn the currency called "Fruits" on the right side of the screen, there are three black bars, when you click on them, the improvement menu will slide out, which has many options:


1. You can exchange "Fruit" for "Money"


2. You can improve the course with which you exchange "Fruit" for "Money", for example, before this improvement you exchange 8 "Fruit" for 1 "Money", but after this improvement you exchange 7 "Fruit" for 1 "Money".


3. You can buy "Farmer" or "Drone" and other things that will passively earn you "Fruit" and you won't have to do anything.


You can see how much "Fruit" or "Money" you have in the upper left corner


In the lower left corner there are two buttons "save game" and "reset game". The "save game" button saves your progress so that when you exit the game and come back, all your progress is not lost, and the "reset game" button restarts all your progress if you want to start from the beginning.
